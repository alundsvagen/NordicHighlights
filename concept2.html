The Nordics built some of the world's most advanced fintech. The Eurozone built the world's largest single market. So why are they still talking past each other?

Nordic operators don't have a capital problem. They have an access problem — access to the distribution, regulatory frameworks, and institutional networks that turn great fintech into continent-scale businesses.

Eurozone investors don't have a deal flow problem. They have a discovery problem — the best-performing, fastest-growing fintechs in Europe are being built north of the Baltic and most of them never hear about it.

HIGHLIGHTS Summit is where that changes. Helsinki sits at the crossroads. The EuroZONE is the room where the bridge gets built — deals get done, partnerships get formed, and capital finds the companies it's been looking for.
